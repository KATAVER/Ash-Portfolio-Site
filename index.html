About Me

I’m a junior majoring in Game Development and Interactive Media and minoring in Disruptive Innovation at the University of Southern California, as well as a proud Hispanic Scholarship Fund Scholar. I’ve been learning about game development and creating games both solo and in teams for three years. Thanks to a program at my school called AGP, I’m familiar with the full development and production cycle of making a game in a short amount of time and the dedication, communication, and coordination it requires. I’ve gained experience in a variety of roles, including Level Designer, Co-Director, VFX and Tech Artist, and most recently, Design Lead on my biggest project yet! When I’m not working on games, you can find me baking at home or practicing MMA at the gym.
